The Easiest and Simplest Fettuccine Alfredo

Ingredients

  • Fettuccine
  • Heavy Whipping Cream
  • Butter
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Water
  • Salt

(You can add a few other ingredients but they are not needed)

Steps:

  1. Start heating up your pan on medium to medium-low heat.
  2. Start boiling water by filling a pot up with water and putting it on high heat.
  3. As your water begins boiling, salt it to taste.
  4. After letting it boil for a couple minutes, add your fettuccine, making sure that everything is covered.
  5. Set a timer for 9-12 minutes - That's when your noodles will be done.
  6. In the pan, put one cup (one stick) of butter.
  7. As your butter melts, add equal amounts of heavy whipping cream (about 1 cup).
  8. Continue to stir on medium to medium-low heat.
  9. As the liquids start to evaporate and thicken, optionally add garlic/garlic powder, black pepper, and any other spices you might want.
  10. About 2-4 minutes before you want to mix everyting together, add equal parts of parmesan cheese (about a cup) and continue to mix continuously.
  11. As it gets close to the 9-12 minutes, start checking your noodles to see if they're cooked enough for your preference.
  12. When your noodles are at your preferred texture, take them out and place it into your pan of butter, cream, and cheese.
  13. Continue mixing the noodles and sauce for a minute or two.
  14. Optionally add extra spices and extra cheese.
  15. Remove from the heat and plate as much or as little as you'd like.
